Preparing for your Tele-Consultation visit

Clothing

Loose-fitting clothing to promote comfort and circulation.
Avoid tight clothing that may restrict blood flow

Lighting

Ensure adequate lighting in the room to avoid glare or shadows on the screen.
Position lights behind or beside the screen, not directly in front.

PC/Laptop

Place your PC/laptop on a stable surface at eye level to reduce strain on your neck and back.
Keep the keyboard and screen clean and free of dust.

Assistance

Ideally, you should have a second person nearby to assist in tasks

What you'll need

  • List of all of your medications and/or current pill bottles

  • If diabetic, list of your glucometer readings

  • If possible, check your blood pressure and weight prior to visit
